QUESTIONS?

Which areas do you serve and are licensed to work?

We are based out of Mastic Beach, New York and offer services to Suffolk and Nassau county. We have a home improvement license for Suffolk county with liability insurance so we are able to design and construct new decks which require a permit. We also provide our services to those based in Nassau county who are interested in improving an already existing deck without changing its footprint.

What sort of coverage do you have/offer?

We have a Home Improvement License as well as General Liability insurance. Aside from warranties that come with the materials and products, we also offer a 1 year warranty in craftsmanship.

I have a project in mind, where do we start?

You can start by getting in contact with us over email at telesecustomdecks@gmail.com, or by calling/texting us at 1(631)-245-4291. For more contact information please fill out our Contact Form.

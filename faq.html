Frequently Asked Questions

Q:

Which areas do you serve and are licensed to work?

A:

We are based out of Mastic Beach, New York and offer services to Suffolk and Nassau county. We have a home improvement license for Suffolk county with liability insurance so we are able to design and construct new decks which require a permit. We also provide our services to those based in Nassau county who are interested in improving an already existing deck without changing its footprint.

Q:

How much should I expect to pay for...?

A:

There are a lot of variables that can affect cost of a project (Materials, Space, Time constraints, etc). After getting in contact we will send someone down to inspect the project area when you are available as to help get a better understanding of what your needs and budget are so that we can make the best design and materials within your budget.

Q:

What sort of coverage do you have/offer?

A:

We have a Home Improvement License as well as General Liability insurance. Aside from warranties that come with the materials and products, we also offer a 1 year warranty in craftsmanship.

Q:

Are decks the only home improvements you offer?

A:

No! We also do other projects as well including fences, renovations (such as doors, windows, crown moulding), and even finishing up previously incomplete carpentry projects.

Q:

How long have you been in the industry?

A:

John started in the construction industry over 20 years ago. The last 10 years of that experience was spent specializing in designing, budgeting, and building decks from the ground up.
